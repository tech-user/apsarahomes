Brief Note

M/s Apsara Constructions is a partnership firm consisting of two partners at present ie Sri. VemulaAnand Kumar and Sri.Survi VenkateshGoud.The firm is mainly into development and construc tion of independent houses,duplexhouses,villas and flats..The firm was registered on 29-5-2012, functioning actively since one year and achieved a turnover of more than Rs. 8.31 crores in just 5 months for the financial year ending March,2013 with a net profit of Rs.14.47 lakhs.The firm has achieved a turn over of Rs.12.63 crores from 1.4.2013 to 30.11.2013 ie in just 8 months with a net profit of Rs.39.19 lakhs. As the number of projects is increasing, a Pvt Ltd company by name M/s ApsaraNextgen Homes India Pvt Ltd was floated with Mr.VAnand Kumar and S.VenkateshGoud as its directors. Earlier Mr .V Anand Kumar was doing construction business for more than a decade in the style of M/s SS Builders with his wife and father as partners. Most of the constructions are in twin cities and the firm has earned good reputation for their quality construction.
